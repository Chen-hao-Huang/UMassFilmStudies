Nedar

Directed by Carla Subirana
Screening Date: April 4, 2017
7:30PM, Integrative Learning Center S404
Run Time: 94 Minutes


(2008, dir Carla Subirana, 94 min)
The fragility of family, history, memory and nation are highlighted as they intertwine in this highly personal film about the lives of three generations of women in the filmmaker’s family pieced together before they are lost and forgotten. In collaboration with the Catalan Film Festival. The director will be present.
